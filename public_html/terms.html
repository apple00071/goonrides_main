Terms & Conditions

By renting a bike from Go On Riders, you agree to the following terms and conditions. These terms are designed to ensure the safety and satisfaction of both the rider and the rental service.

1. Rental Terms

The rental period begins at the time of bike pickup and ends when the bike is returned to our location. Late returns will incur additional charges as per the hourly rate.

2. Security Deposit

A refundable security deposit is required at the time of rental. The deposit amount varies based on the bike model and rental duration. The deposit will be refunded upon safe return of the bike.

3. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 24 hours before the rental period will receive a full refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before will incur a 50% cancellation fee.

4. Fuel Policy

Bikes are provided with a full tank of fuel and should be returned with the same amount. If returned with less fuel, the difference will be charged at current market rates.

Helmets are mandatory and provided free of charge. Riders are responsible for any loss or damage to safety equipment.

6. Damage and Liability

The renter is responsible for any damage to the bike during the rental period. Insurance coverage is available at additional cost.

7. Eligibility Requirements

Riders must be at least 18 years of age and possess a valid driver's license. Original ID proof and license must be presented at the time of rental.

8. Usage Restrictions

Rented bikes cannot be used for racing, stunts, or any illegal activities. Bikes should not be taken outside Hyderabad city limits without prior permission.
