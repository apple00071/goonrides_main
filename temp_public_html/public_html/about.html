ABOUT US

We, at City Bike Rental Services, are into two wheeler rental operation since 2017. We are the pioneer in this service in Hyderabad. As per present scenario of heavy traffic jam and people running short of time, it is important to have easy mode of transportation and more importantly to have easy access of this mode of transport. When we thought about this problem, we started surveying people coming from different background and different locations. We found that two wheeler is easy to handle and park, fuel efficient and easy to move anywhere across the city. We started our journey in year 2017
